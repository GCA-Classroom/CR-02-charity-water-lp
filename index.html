Why Clean Water?

703 million people in the world live without clean water. It’s a crisis that affects health, education, and opportunity — especially for women and children.

Charity: water is on a mission to change that. We fund sustainable water solutions in communities that need it most. 100% of public donations go directly to the field.

When you give, you’re not just donating — you’re helping end the water crisis in our lifetime.
